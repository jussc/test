Fees

$120/50 min (sliding scale available, limited spaces- please inquire).

  • Acceptable forms of payment: cash, check or credit card. Payment is due at time of service.
  • We do not accept Insurance. Managed care/insurance companies require a diagnosis as well as determine the type of therapy and number of sessions you are allowed. We prefer to use a more individualized approach, to best suit your needs. Insurance companies can compromise your privacy and confidentiality, since the information they retain becomes a part of your permanent record. However, we can provide superbills, which may help with insurance reimbursement (please inquire with your insurance company directly).
  • PLEASE NOTE there is a strict CANCELLATION POLICY: 24-hour notice is required. Full fee is charged for no-shows or late cancellations.
